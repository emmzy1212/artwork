Art Investment Strategies: Advising clients on building investment portfolios that include art assets. This involves analyzing market trends, assessing risk factors, and developing strategies to maximize returns on art investments over time.

Art Market Research : Conducting in-depth research on specific artists, art movements, or regions of the art market to identify emerging trends and opportunities. This research helps clients make informed decisions about buying and selling artworks.

Corporate Art Programs : Developing customized art programs for corporations, including art acquisitions, exhibitions, and employee engagement initiatives. These programs enhance corporate culture, support local artists, and create inspiring work environments.

Consulting : Offering personalized advice and guidance on all aspects of the art world, from building a collection to navigating legal and ethical issues. This can include educational workshops, seminars, and one-on-one consultations.
